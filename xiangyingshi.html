WHO LEADS OUR TRAINING?

Stuff & Nonsense is one of the UK’s best known web design studios and is directed by Andy Clarke. Andy has over twenty years experience designing for the web. He’s worked with clients including Disney Store, ISO, WIPO and WWF UK.

Andy’s an accomplished speaker and a teacher, published author who has written three widely acclaimed books about designing for the web, including Transcending CSS (2005,) Hardboiled Web Design (2010) and Hardboiled Web Design Fifth Anniversary Edition (2015.) He’s given over sixty presentations about design and digital design workflow at conferences all around the world since 2005.
